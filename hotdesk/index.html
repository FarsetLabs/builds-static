Hot-Desking


Farset Labs is a great place to get some work done in the city, or a place to meet up with colleagues and friends, and with on site tea/coffee facilities, 150Mbps fibre internet, and access to our prototyping workshops, Farset Labs is a great place to develop, create, and collaborate.

Visitor passes are not available while the building is closed (COVID-19)

All Day Pass dues go towards the core operation of the space; events and projects are normally funded by additional donations / sponsorships.
